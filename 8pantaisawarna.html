Pantai Sawarna

Source: Courtesy of Google

Pantai Sawarna is a popular tourist destination located in Bayah, Lebak, Banten, Indonesia. It is known for its scenic beauty, long stretches of white sandy beaches, and clear blue waters.

Pantai Sawarna is actually a collection of several beaches, each with its own unique characteristics. The most famous of these beaches is Tanjung Layar, which is named after a nearby rock formation that looks like a ship's sail. This beach is known for its spectacular sunsets and is a popular spot for surfing, although the waves can be quite strong and challenging for beginners.

Another beach in the area is Pantai Karang Bokor, which is famous for its rock formations and tidal pools. Visitors can explore the pools during low tide and see a variety of marine life such as crabs, starfish, and small fish.

Pantai Sawarna is also a popular destination for those who enjoy hiking and exploring nature. There are several hiking trails in the area, including one that leads to the top of Mount Sawarna, which offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Overall, Pantai Sawarna is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves the beach, surfing, hiking, and exploring the natural beauty of Indonesia.
