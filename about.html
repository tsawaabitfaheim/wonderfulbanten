About Us

We Provide 10 Best Tourism Destinations

Banten is a province located in the western part of the Indonesian island of Java. It offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty that makes it an ideal destination for travelers seeking a diverse experience. Some of the top attractions in Banten include the historic port city of Old Banten, the scenic Ujung Kulon National Park, the beautiful beaches of Anyer and Carita, and the traditional villages of Baduy. Other popular activities include exploring the Thousand Islands, hiking up the iconic Mount Krakatoa, and indulging in the delicious local cuisine. Whether you're interested in history, nature, or simply relaxing on the beach, Banten has something for everyone to enjoy.
