Arung Jeram Sungai Ciberang

Short Description

Arum Jeram Sungai Ciberang

Source: Courtesy of Google

Arung Jeram Sungai Ciberang is a popular white water rafting destination located in the Lebak Regency of Banten Province in Indonesia. The river flows through the scenic hills and valleys of the Gunung Halimun-Salak National Park, providing an exciting and thrilling adventure for visitors.

The river is divided into several sections of varying difficulty levels, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced rafters. The rapids range from Class I to Class III, offering a mix of challenging and enjoyable rides for rafting enthusiasts.

The journey down the river takes approximately 2-3 hours, during which visitors can enjoy the breathtaking natural beauty of the surrounding landscape, including lush forests, cascading waterfalls, and towering cliffs. Along the way, visitors can also take a dip in the crystal-clear river, which is cool and refreshing even on the hottest days

Arung Jeram Sungai Ciberang is a well-maintained and safe rafting destination, with experienced guides and modern equipment. Visitors are provided with safety gear such as helmets and life jackets, and are given a thorough briefing on rafting techniques and safety procedures before setting out.

In addition to white water rafting, visitors can also enjoy other activities in the area such as hiking, camping, and bird watching. The nearby Gunung Halimun-Salak National Park is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including rare and endangered species such as the Javan gibbon and the Java leopard.

Overall, Arung Jeram Sungai Ciberang is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for an adrenaline-filled adventure in the midst of stunning natural beauty.
