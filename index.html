About Us

Dear travellers,

Welcome to Banten, Indonesia! Nestled between the bustling city of Jakarta and the tranquil beaches of the West Java coast, Banten offers a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. Explore the rich heritage of Banten through its many historical landmarks, such as the majestic Great Mosque of Banten and the imposing Krakatoa Monument. Take a step back in time and visit the charming colonial town of Anyer, where remnants of the Dutch East Indies Company can still be seen. For nature enthusiasts, Banten has plenty to offer.

Go on a thrilling adventure and climb the active volcano of Mount Krakatoa, or relax on the pristine beaches of Carita and Tanjung Lesung. Take a dip in the warm waters of the Sunda Strait, and spot exotic marine life while snorkeling or scuba diving. But the true gem of Banten is its people. The warm and friendly locals will welcome you with open arms and show you the true meaning of Indonesian hospitality. And the best part? Banten is easily accessible from Jakarta, making it the perfect destination for a weekend getaway or a longer vacation. So what are you waiting for? Come and discover the hidden treasures of Banten, Indonesia.

Book your trip today and create memories that will last a lifetime. We can't wait to welcome you to our beautiful province!
